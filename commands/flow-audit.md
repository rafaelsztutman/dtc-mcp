---
name: flow-audit
description: Review flows for optimization opportunities
---

Using `klaviyo_flow_summary` for all live flows, audit the flow program:

1. **Core Flow Check**: Verify these 5 essential flows exist:
   - Welcome Series
   - Abandoned Cart
   - Browse Abandonment
   - Post-Purchase
   - Winback
   Flag any missing flows as immediate opportunities.

2. **Performance Review**: For each active flow:
   - Revenue attribution and conversion rates
   - Compare against other flows in the account
   - Identify flows with declining performance

3. **Deep Dive**: Use `klaviyo_flow_detail` on the top 3 revenue-generating flows to analyze per-message performance and identify drop-off points.

4. **Recommendations**: Prioritized list of optimizations based on revenue impact potential.
