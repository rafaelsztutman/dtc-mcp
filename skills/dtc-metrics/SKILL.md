# DTC Metrics Reference

Key metrics for DTC e-commerce brands, in order of importance:

## Revenue Metrics
- **Revenue**: Total sales in period
- **AOV** (Average Order Value): revenue / orders
- **RPR** (Revenue per Recipient): Email revenue / emails delivered. Better than total revenue for campaign comparison.
- **LTV** (Customer Lifetime Value): Total revenue from a customer over their lifetime

## Acquisition Metrics
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost): Total marketing spend / new customers acquired
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Target >3:1 for sustainable growth
- **New vs Returning Split**: Healthy = 30-40% new customers

## Email/SMS Benchmarks
| Metric | Healthy | Warning | Critical |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| Open Rate | >25% | 20-25% | <20% |
| Click Rate | >3% | 2-3% | <2% |
| Unsubscribe Rate | <0.2% | 0.2-0.3% | >0.3% |
| Spam Rate | <0.05% | 0.05-0.1% | >0.1% |
| Bounce Rate | <1% | 1-2% | >2% |

## Email Revenue Share
| Range | Assessment |
|-------|------------|
| 25-40% of total | Healthy email program |
| <20% of total | Underperforming — optimize flows and campaigns |
| >50% of total | Over-reliant — diversify acquisition channels |

## When Presenting Metrics
1. Show the number AND the benchmark context
2. Include period-over-period comparison
3. Flag anything outside healthy ranges
4. Lead with revenue impact, not vanity metrics
